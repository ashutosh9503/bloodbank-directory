About Our Mission

Bloodbank Directory India is a community-driven initiative designed to bridge the gap between donors and patients in need. Our goal is to provide a unified, accessible, and real-time database of blood banks across the nation.

How it works

  • We aggregate publicly available data from government and private sources.
  • Users can search by location, state, or specific blood bank type.
  • We provide direct contact numbers and map locations for immediate action.
